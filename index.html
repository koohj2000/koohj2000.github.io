Ph.D. Candidate in Statistics

Research Publications Teaching

I develop interpretable, scalable, and reproducible statistical methods for high-dimensional unsupervised learning, with a focus on convex clustering, regularization, and optimization-based model design. My work examines how structured penalties shape clustering behavior and statistical stability, and is motivated by applications across spatial transcriptomics, multi-omic, microbiome, and other complex biological datasets.
