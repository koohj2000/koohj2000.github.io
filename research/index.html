Overview

My research focuses on developing statistically principled, optimization-driven methods for unsupervised learning. I currently work on convex clustering, structured regularization, and algorithmic frameworks that ensure interpretability, stability, and reproducibility in high-dimensional settings.

Current Projects

  • Angle-based clustering (with A. J. Molstad)
  • Multi-sample clustering — developing a framework that jointly models shared and sample-specific structure across related datasets (with A. J. Molstad)
