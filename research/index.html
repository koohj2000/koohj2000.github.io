Overview

My research focuses on developing statistically principled, optimization-driven methods for unsupervised learning. I work on convex clustering, structured regularization, and algorithmic frameworks that ensure interpretability, stability, and reproducibility in high-dimensional settings.

Current Projects

  • Scale-invariant clustering and deconvolution (with A. J. Molstad)
  • Angle-based clustering (with A. J. Molstad)
  • Constrained convex clustering for interpretable spatial domain detection
